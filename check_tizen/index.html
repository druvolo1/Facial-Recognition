This application demonstrates the use of B2B RTP Player plugin


Operation

Use mouse or remote to operate on video player.

1.- Type the URL from the stream. Example: "udp://239.10.10.40:29001" and click Load URL

2.- Press Play from the remote or click on "Play" with the mouse.

KEY 0: to clear logs.

To stop the content: Press Stop from the remote or click on "Stop" with the mouse.

To change to Fullscreen: Press ENTER from the remote or click on "TOGGLE FULLSCREEN MODE" with the mouse.
