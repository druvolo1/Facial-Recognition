Tips for Better Face Recognition

  • Lighting: Ensure good, even lighting on your face. Avoid backlighting or harsh shadows.
  • Position: Keep your face centered in the frame throughout the process.
  • Distance: Stay 1-2 feet from the camera.
  • Follow guidance: The system will guide you through different head positions automatically.
  • Move smoothly: Turn your head slowly and smoothly when prompted.
  • Remove obstacles: Take off sunglasses. Minimize glare if wearing glasses.
  • Stay still: Hold each position steady when capturing.
RECORDING
The process takes about 10-15 seconds. Follow the on-screen instructions to move your head to different angles.
