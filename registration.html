Tips for Better Face Recognition

  • Lighting: Ensure good, even lighting on your face. Avoid backlighting or harsh shadows.
  • Position: Look directly at the camera. Keep your face centered and fill most of the frame.
  • Distance: Stay 1-2 feet from the camera for optimal capture.
  • Angles: Capture photos from slightly different angles (frontal, slight left, slight right).
  • Expression: Maintain a neutral expression with eyes open and clearly visible.
  • Remove obstacles: Take off sunglasses. If wearing regular glasses, adjust to minimize glare.
  • Background: Use a plain, uncluttered background when possible.
  • Stay still: Hold steady during capture to avoid blur.
